InstaCart

Accomplishments Lessons Learned
  • Data Organization: Extracted and organized transactional data from multiple tables for further analysis.

  • Database Management: Created and managed a comprehensive database on MS Access for the Instacart transactional data.

  • Data Analysis: Performed in-depth analysis of customer behaviors and preferences based on the transactional data.

  • Insight Generation: Generated insights on customer reordering patterns, first-time orders, the sequence of items added to the cart, and timing of orders.

  • Predictive Models: Developed models that predict customer buying habits to enhance the customer shopping experience.

  • Reporting: Documented all findings in a comprehensive report, detailing the process and results of the data analysis.
  • Data Management: Gained experience in handling large and complex datasets, extracting useful data, and organizing it effectively for analysis.

  • MS Access Skills: Enhanced proficiency in using MS Access for managing databases and conducting data analysis.

  • Data Analysis: Improved understanding of how to analyze transactional data and draw meaningful insights from it.

  • Customer Behavior: Gained insights into customer behavior in online shopping environments and learned about different factors that influence customer buying habits.

  • Predictive Modeling: Learned the importance of predictive modeling in business and how to develop these models.

  • Communication of Findings: Improved skills in documenting findings and communicating them effectively in a report.
