Featured Projects

Title: Natural By Design - Project Administration System In my Advanced Databases course, I undertook a comprehensive project to create an efficient project administration system for a hypothetical company, "Natural By Design". This task required in-depth knowledge of entity-relationship (ER) modeling and SQL, and was aimed at managing the complex interdependencies inherent in design projects.

Title: Performance Lawn Equipment Case Study - The project examined operational performance and employee retention at Performance Lawn Equipment (PLE), focusing on product defect rates (2014-2018) and employee retention factors such as tenure, education, GPA, age, and gender. Using statistical techniques, numerical findings were interpreted into actionable insights for PLE's non-technical management, to aid strategic decision-making.

Title: Analyzing Customer Behavior using Instacart Transactional Data - This individual assignment required the analysis of customer transactional data from Instacart, a grocery ordering and delivery app. Through the process exporting data excel files to Microsoft Access. Detailed analyses were performed on these data segments, focusing on factors like shopping cart sequence, reorder status, user identification, order timing, and frequency. The study aimed to predict user behavior, including product reordering and first-time purchases. By understanding these customer patterns, the assignment intended to contribute towards enhancing the shopping experience offered by Instacart.

Title: Advanced Excel: Strategic Decision-Making - The advanced Excel assignment involved an extensive exercise in data manipulation and analysis, simulating strategic decision-making processes that businesses regularly undertake. The tasks covered a wide range of Excel functionalities such as file renaming, data sorting, vLookup application, computations, income statement completion, pivot table and chart creation, scenario, and solver analysis. These activities provided an opportunity to interact with Excel's features, interpret and draw meaningful insights from raw data, thus enhancing analytical skills and business understanding.
